    ABOUT





    Welcome to my personal website.
    RMZ is a musical project mixing Jazz, Hip-Hop and Soul.

    I am a 23yo multi-instrumentalist, producer and vocalist.
    Most songs you will hear are composed, recorded and mixed
    in my bedroom.
    No record deal, no label and no one telling me what to do ;
    this is a fully self driven and independent project.

    I enjoy creating new songs, producing beats and
    inviting friends to feature on my records.
    My first EP Opening Lines came out January 2018.
    It is a self produced record featuring my good friend
    Nicolas Klein on the bass.

    I am now working on my next project named Section 8 Groove
    which should be ready soon for a preview.

    Thank you for passing by my website and enjoy the music.

    RMZ
